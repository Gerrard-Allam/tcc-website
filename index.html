What it is

A team building experience where everyone's involved.

The Craft Collective is a hands-on team building experience. Your team splits into Makers, who build cocktails, and Judges, who score them, with a live leaderboard tracking the standings until the final round decides the winner.

The format

Everything handled. You just show up.

Shown here: the format of our flagship package, The Collective Cup.

Live demo

The 101

The facilitator builds one cocktail from scratch, live, before anyone touches a station.

Round 1

The Classic

Teams replicate the demo cocktail. First scores hit the leaderboard.

Round 2

The Build

Judges order from a cheat sheet of cocktails and mocktails. Makers build to order.

Round 3

The Signature

Full creative freedom. Teams name their own cocktail and pitch it to the judges.

Who's behind it

Built by someone who has done both sides of this.
