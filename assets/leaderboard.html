The format
You are a maker or a judge — check with your facilitator if unsure.
Makers work in pods. Name your pod at the start.
Round 1: replicate The 101 cocktail with one creative choice.
Round 2: a surprise ingredient is revealed. It must go in your cocktail.
Round 3: full creative freedom. Name it, build it, pitch it in 30 seconds.
The maker–judge relationship
Ask your judge what they want before you build.
Read them. Build something for them, not just for the score.
Ask if they want it as a cocktail or mocktail — every time.
Judges: score honestly. The Palate Award goes to the sharpest palate, not the most generous one.

The Collective Cup — winning maker pod.
