The walkthrough below follows The Collective Cup, our flagship three-round format. The Session and The Collective run a shorter version of the same structure.

Before the event

Three steps. None of them complicated.

01

Book and confirm

Choose your package and date. You'll receive a confirmation within 48 hours with next steps to lock it in. A 50% deposit secures your date.

02

Receive the spirits list

10 days before your event, we send a curated shopping list for base spirits. Your team picks up the bottles, we handle everything else.

03

We set up everything

The facilitator arrives 60 minutes early with all equipment, ingredients, scoring materials, and event signage. Your team walks into a ready room.

The stages

Five stages. One arc of escalating excitement.

The format is back-loaded by design. Makers work in pods of two, with up to two pods per station and up to three stations running side by side. Each round raises the stakes. By Round 3, anyone can win.

The Collective Cup run of show
The 10120 min

Live Demo

Facilitator only

The facilitator builds one cocktail from scratch in front of the room. Every tool, every decision, every technique narrated live. This becomes the reference point for Round 1. No one is scored. Everyone pays attention.

Round 140 min

The Classic

Replicate the 101

Makers replicate the 101 cocktail, focusing on the technique and tips observed in The 101 round. Judges score each cocktail using a criteria card covering balance, presentation, and technique. First scores go on the live leaderboard.

Round 240 min

The Build

Judges order, makers deliver

Judges order from a cheat sheet of cocktails and mocktails. Makers deliver to order, drawing on the technique and range built across the first round. Scores update live. Leaderboard positions shift.

Round 340 min

The Signature

Full creative freedom

Makers build completely from scratch, name the cocktail, and pitch it to their judges in 30 seconds. Anyone can win going in. The stakes are highest.

Worth more than Rounds 1 and 2 combined
Close20 min

Awards

Ceremony for the two awards: Makers / Judges

Two awards are presented. The Collective Cup goes to the winning maker pod. The Palate Award goes to the judge pair whose scores tracked closest to the panel consensus, rewarding accuracy over generosity.

Live Leaderboard

Displayed in the room, updated round by round as scores come in.

The two tracks

Two tracks, side by side. One room. Everyone involved.

The Craft Collective is the only format in the DC market running two structured tracks at once. Makers and judges are both being scored, at the same time, in the same room, for different prizes.

Makers track

Build. Score. Win.

Work in pairs to build cocktails across three rounds, judged on technique, presentation, and creativity. The pod with the highest cumulative score across all three rounds wins.

Award
The Collective Cup
Trophy, engraved jigger, and a custom labelled bottle.
Judges track

Score. Calibrate. Win.

Work in pairs and score every cocktail together on the same criteria. The pair whose scores land closest to the panel consensus across all three rounds wins, rewarding accuracy over generosity.

Award
The Palate Award
Awarded to the judge pair with the lowest cumulative deviation from the panel.
