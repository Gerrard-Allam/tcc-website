Privacy Policy.

The Craft Collective LLC — Washington DC, Maryland, Virginia

Last updated: July 16, 2026

What We Collect

When you submit an inquiry through this website (including our Book a Session form, Private Celebrations form, or general contact form), we collect the information you provide directly, which may include your name, email address, phone number, company name, and details about your event.

We do not use tracking cookies, analytics pixels, or third-party advertising scripts on this site beyond what is required for the site itself to function.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiry and discuss your event
  • Send you a proposal, quote, or booking confirmation
  • Coordinate logistics for a booked event
  • Send you a receipt or invoice if you book with us

We do not use your information for marketing unrelated to your inquiry, and we do not send unsolicited email.

How Your Information Is Stored

Form submissions are processed through Formspree, a third-party form service, and delivered to us by email. We do not maintain a separate marketing database of website visitors.

Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We do not disclose your information to third parties except:

  • Where required by law
  • To service providers who help us operate (such as our form processor), who are only permitted to use your information to provide that service

Your Choices

You may contact us at any time to ask what information we have on file for you, to correct it, or to request that we delete it, subject to any records we're required to retain for legitimate business or legal purposes (such as invoices).

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to thecraftcollective.info@gmail.com.

This policy applies to thecraftcollectivedc.com and covers information collected through this website only.

Your information is never disclosed to third parties.
